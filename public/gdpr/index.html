This page summarises how ApplyCraft handles personal data under the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and comparable laws. It complements the Privacy Policy.

1. Controller

The data controller for applycraft.io is Biroue Digital Ltd. Contact: hello@applycraft.io.

2. What we process and why

  • Resume / cover letter content — processed in your browser to build your document. Not stored on our servers unless you opt into cloud sync. Legal basis: performing the service you request.
  • Account email + Master Profile (only if you opt into sync) — stored to sync across your devices. Legal basis: your consent and performance of the optional feature.
  • Text you submit to an AI helper — sent to our AI subprocessor only when you click an AI action. Legal basis: your request.
  • Server logs (IP, user agent, timestamp) — retained briefly by our host for security. Legal basis: legitimate interest in protecting the service.
  • Aggregated analytics — cookieless, non-identifying usage counts. Legal basis: legitimate interest in improving the product.

3. Subprocessors

  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, and security (global, incl. US/EU).
  • Anthropic — the AI model behind optional AI helpers (US). See the AI Disclosure.
  • Lemon Squeezy — payments / merchant of record for the optional pass (US).
  • Plausible Analytics — cookieless analytics (EU-hosted).
  • Google Fonts — font delivery.

4. International transfers

Some subprocessors are based outside the EEA/UK (for example, in the United States). Where personal data is transferred internationally, it is covered by the relevant safeguards those providers offer, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or applicable data-transfer frameworks.

5. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection to processing, and you may withdraw consent at any time. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6. Exercising your rights

For account and cloud data, use "Delete my saved data" in the app to erase your account, stored Master Profile, and session. For any other request, email hello@applycraft.io. We respond within 72 hours and may need to verify your identity.
