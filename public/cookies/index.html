This page explains the cookies and browser storage ApplyCraft relies on. The core builder is designed to avoid tracking cookies — but it is not accurate to say "no cookies at all," because our security provider and optional features use a small number of necessary technologies.

1. Local storage, not cookies, for your work

Your resume draft, saved resumes, language choice, and (if you opt in) account session are kept in your browser's localStorage — not in cookies, and not sent to our servers by default. You can clear them with the "Delete local data" button in the app or via your browser settings. Keys we use include:

  • ac_resume_draft, ac_resumes, ac_current_resume_id — your resume content and selection
  • ac_subscription, ac_session, ac_account, ac_consent_sync — optional account / pass state

2. Analytics is cookieless

We use Plausible Analytics, which is cookieless and collects only anonymous, aggregated events (for example, "a resume was exported"). It sets no cookies and does not track you across sites. See the Privacy Policy.

4. Managing cookies and storage

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings and clear ApplyCraft's local storage with the in-app "Delete local data" control. Blocking strictly necessary security cookies may affect the site's ability to protect against abuse.
