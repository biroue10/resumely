How it works

What this checker actually measures

Five categories that determine how well your resume performs with ATS software.

Contact

Contact completeness

Email, phone, and LinkedIn URL. ATS systems extract these to build your candidate profile — missing any reduces your parseable score.

Structure

Section detection

Experience, education, and skills sections must be clearly labeled so parsers can identify and rank them correctly.

Numbers

Quantification

Bullets with metrics (%, $, headcount, time) rank higher and signal impact. At least one number per role is the baseline.

Dates

Date formatting

ATS systems calculate tenure and flag gaps using year ranges. Roles without dates create parsing errors and reduce completeness score.

Keywords

Keyword match

When you paste a job description, we tokenize both documents and show the exact overlap and gaps. Target 40%+ for competitive roles.

Common questions

What is an ATS resume checker?

An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) checker analyzes your resume for the signals automated hiring software uses to rank candidates — contact completeness, keyword density, quantified achievements, date formatting, and structural clarity. Most mid-to-large companies use ATS software to filter applications before a human ever reads them.

Is this ATS checker really free?

Yes — completely free, no sign-up, no email address, no credit card. Your resume text runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing is ever sent to a server or stored anywhere.

How should I read the ApplyCraft ATS Readiness Score?

Treat it as a guide, not a verdict. Within ApplyCraft's heuristic, a higher score means fewer structural, content, and keyword issues were found; a lower score points to fixable gaps worth addressing. It is not a universal industry threshold and does not predict whether you'll get an interview. When you paste a job description, the keyword-match percentage shows how much of that role's vocabulary your resume already contains — useful for tailoring honestly.

Does this tool guarantee I'll pass ATS?

No. Every ATS system is different — Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, Taleo and other platforms use different parsing and ranking criteria. This checker identifies common patterns that can hurt ATS readability, but there is no universal guarantee.

What's the best way to use the keyword gap feature?

Paste the exact job posting — including the full description, not just the title. Then add the missing keywords naturally into your experience and skills sections, but only if they genuinely reflect your background. Never fabricate experience to hit keywords; a tailored resume that's honest is both more ethical and more effective when you reach the interview stage.

Can I check a resume in French, Arabic, or Spanish?

Yes. The structural and quantification checks work in any language. The keyword matching compares your resume against whatever job description you paste, so multilingual matching works automatically. ApplyCraft's full builder supports 99 document languages with live preview and PDF/DOCX export.
