About these states

We report each component as Operational, Degraded performance, Partial outage, or Major outage. ApplyCraft components are listed separately from third-party providers (Cloudflare, Anthropic, Lemon Squeezy), whose own status pages are linked above.

We do not claim a specific uptime percentage and do not present Cloudflare's infrastructure SLA as ApplyCraft's measured uptime. The setup for independent, measured monitoring is documented in the repository (docs/STATUS_MONITORING.md).
