The only paid item on ApplyCraft is the optional, one-time Active Search Pass (US$7, non-recurring). Everything else is free. This page explains refunds for that purchase.

1. What you are buying

The Active Search Pass unlocks AI tailoring and cross-device Master Profile sync for 7 days. It is a one-time purchase that does not auto-renew. Documents you create and download remain yours after the pass expires; only the pass-only features stop.

2. Merchant of record

Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, which is the merchant of record and handles billing, taxes, and refund processing. We do not receive or store your full card details.

3. Requesting a refund

Because the pass grants immediate access to digital features, please contact us before or shortly after purchase if something is wrong. To request a refund, email hello@applycraft.io from the address used at checkout, within 14 days of purchase, including your Lemon Squeezy order number. We will review genuine issues (for example, the pass features did not work) and, where appropriate, arrange a refund through Lemon Squeezy.

4. Statutory rights

If you are a consumer in the EU, UK, or another jurisdiction with a statutory withdrawal ("cooling-off") period for digital content, those rights apply. Where you ask us to begin providing the digital features immediately, the right to withdraw may end once delivery has begun, to the extent permitted by law.

5. Contact

Refund questions: hello@applycraft.io. We typically respond within 72 hours.
