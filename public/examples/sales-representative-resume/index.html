How to write a sales representative resume

Sales resumes live and die on numbers. Quota attainment, revenue, and ranking matter more than responsibilities — lead with them.

🎯

Lead With Quota Attainment

'128% of quota' or 'top 5% of reps' in your summary and bullets immediately signals you can sell.

💵

Quantify Revenue & Pipeline

Deal sizes, revenue closed, pipeline generated, and territory growth are the metrics hiring managers scan for first.

🛠️

List Your CRM & Tools

Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Salesloft, Gong — CRM and sales-engagement tools are common explicit requirements.

🏆

Show Rankings & Awards

President's Club, top-rep rankings, and stretch-goal achievements are credible, comparable proof points.

🔁

Describe Your Sales Motion

Inbound vs. outbound, deal cycle length, average deal size, and the industries you sold into add useful context.

✅

Keep It ATS-Readable

A clean single-column layout ensures your metrics and tools are parsed correctly by applicant tracking systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should a sales representative resume include?

A metrics-driven summary, quota attainment and revenue figures, CRM and sales tools, your sales motion (inbound/outbound, deal size, cycle), awards or rankings, and education.

How do I show sales numbers without revealing confidential data?

Use percentages and ranges — 'achieved 128% of quota', 'grew pipeline ~60%' — rather than disclosing exact internal revenue or named client figures.

What if I'm changing into sales from another field?

Translate transferable wins into sales language: targets met, people persuaded, relationships built, and any revenue or growth you influenced. Highlight communication, resilience, and CRM familiarity.

How long should a sales resume be?

One page is ideal and reflects the concise, results-focused style sales leaders expect. Two pages only if you have extensive, directly relevant selling history.
