Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a cover letter in 2026?

Yes — 83% of hiring managers say a strong cover letter influences their decision to interview a candidate. It's your chance to explain your motivation and stand out from applicants with similar CVs.

How long should a cover letter be?

Three to four short paragraphs — roughly 250–400 words. Hiring managers spend less than 30 seconds on a first read, so be concise and compelling.

Should my cover letter match my resume design?

Yes. A consistent look signals attention to detail and professionalism. ApplyCraft's cover letter templates are designed to pair visually with the resume templates.

Can I use the same cover letter for multiple jobs?

Only if you personalise the key details — the company name, role title, and one specific reason why you want to work there. Generic letters are easy for recruiters to spot.
