What's free vs. what needs the pass

Building, previewing, and downloading resumes and cover letters — including every template, document-language controls, RTL-aware layouts, and the ATS checker — are free and require no account. The pass only unlocks two optional extras: AI tailoring and cross-device cloud sync of your Master Profile.

Price, currency & taxes

US$7, charged once. The pass is not a subscription and does not auto-renew. Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy as merchant of record, which applies any sales tax/VAT required for your location at checkout. We never receive or store your full card details.

Limits

AI features and cloud sync are provided under reasonable fair-use limits so the service stays available and affordable for everyone; automated or abusive usage may be rate-limited. We don't publish a fixed per-day quota, and we don't throttle normal, genuine job-search usage.

Account requirement

The free builder needs no account. Cloud sync (part of the pass) uses an optional, passwordless account (sign-in by emailed link) so your Master Profile can follow you across devices.

When the pass expires

After 7 days the pass features (AI tailoring, cloud sync) simply switch off. Nothing you already made is taken away:

• Your downloaded files stay yours — expiry doesn't touch anything you've saved to your device.
• Local data stays in your browser and the free builder keeps working exactly as before.
• Cloud data (if you used sync) is retained so you can renew later, and you can delete it any time with "Delete my saved data" in the app or by emailing us.
