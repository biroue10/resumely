ApplyCraft is designed to handle as little of your data as possible. The core builder and export run in your browser, and several features are strictly optional. This page explains, accurately, what is processed in each case.

⚡ Short version: Your resume is edited in your browser and is not stored on our servers by default. We use cookieless analytics and necessary security cookies (not ad tracking). Optional features — an account with cloud sync, AI helpers, and the paid pass — involve a third party only when you choose to use them.

1. Who we are

ApplyCraft is an independent product built and operated by Biroue Digital Ltd, the data controller, reachable at hello@applycraft.io. The website is at applycraft.io.

2. What data we process

2.1 Resume and cover letter content

By default, your resume and cover letter content is edited and stored only in your own browser (in localStorage). ApplyCraft does not require an account, and this content is not sent to or stored on our servers unless you explicitly use an optional feature below (cloud sync in 2.2, or an AI helper in section 4).

2.2 Optional account and Master Profile sync

You may optionally provide an email address to save your Master Profile and sync it across your devices. This is entirely optional — the builder, all templates, and PDF/DOCX export work fully without it.

  • Sign-in is passwordless: we email you a one-time link. We store your email address and, with your explicit consent, your Master Profile content so it can be synced.
  • Cross-device Master Profile sync is part of the optional paid pass (see section 5). The free builder never requires an account.
  • You can delete all of this at any time using "Delete my saved data" in the app, or by emailing us. Deletion removes your account, stored Master Profile, and session.

2.3 Uploaded files

If you upload a PDF or DOCX with the "improve an existing resume" feature, the text is read in your browser to pre-fill the form. Avoid uploading sensitive documents if you are using a modified build or third-party mirror of ApplyCraft.

2.4 Technical logs

Our hosting and CDN provider (Cloudflare) records standard server access logs — IP address, browser type, URL requested, and timestamp — for security and abuse prevention. We do not use these logs for advertising or to build a profile of you.

3. Cookies and analytics

ApplyCraft sets no advertising cookies and uses no cross-site tracking (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no Hotjar, no ad networks). Product analytics is provided by Plausible, which is cookieless and records only anonymous, aggregated events (such as "a resume was exported") with no names, emails, or document content.

Cloudflare may set strictly necessary security cookies (for example __cf_bm) for bot and abuse protection. Your work is kept in localStorage, not cookies. Full details are in the Cookie Policy.

4. AI features and your data

Optional AI helpers — generating or rewriting content, translation, ATS suggestions, and AI tailoring to a job description — send the text relevant to that action to our AI provider only when you click. The provider is Anthropic (the Claude model family), reached through a server proxy we operate on Cloudflare. The core builder, previews, and export never use AI. Do not submit content you are not comfortable having processed by a third party. Full details are in the AI Disclosure.

5. Payments (optional paid pass)

The optional one-time "Active Search Pass" (US$7, non-recurring) unlocks AI tailoring and Master Profile cloud sync for 7 days. Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, the merchant of record, which handles billing and applicable taxes. We do not receive or store your full card details; we record only whether your pass is active and when it expires. See the Refund Policy.

6. International data transfers

Some providers we rely on are based outside the EEA/UK (for example, Anthropic and Lemon Squeezy in the United States). Where personal data is transferred internationally, it is covered by the safeguards those providers offer, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or applicable data-transfer frameworks. See the GDPR page for the full subprocessor list.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law (including the EU/UK GDPR), you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection, and may withdraw consent at any time. You also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority.

  • Account & cloud data — erase it yourself with "Delete my saved data" in the app, which removes your account, stored Master Profile, and session.
  • Local data — clear it with the in-app "Delete local data" control or your browser settings.
  • Everything else — email hello@applycraft.io and we will action your request.

8. Data retention

The free, no-account builder stores your work only in your browser, which you control. If you opt into Master Profile sync, we retain your email and stored Master Profile until you delete them. Cloudflare access logs are short-lived and used only for security. If you contact us by email, your message is retained only as long as needed to respond.

9. Children's privacy

ApplyCraft is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

10. Third-party services

  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, and security. Privacy policy.
  • Anthropic — AI model behind optional AI helpers. Privacy policy.
  • Lemon Squeezy — payments / merchant of record for the optional pass. Privacy policy.
  • Plausible Analytics — cookieless product analytics. Privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — typography served from Google's CDN. Privacy policy.

11. Security

All traffic to applycraft.io is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. The free builder holds no personal data on our servers. For opted-in accounts we store only your email and Master Profile content, which you can delete at any time.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. Continued use after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact

Privacy questions, concerns, or data requests: hello@applycraft.io. We typically respond within 72 hours.
