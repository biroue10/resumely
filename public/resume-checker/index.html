What does the AI resume checker look for?

Our AI evaluates your resume across the same criteria that ATS systems and professional recruiters use.

🤖

ATS Compatibility

Checks for tables, graphics, and non-standard fonts that cause ATS parsers to misread your resume.

🏷️

Keyword Coverage

Compares your resume against common requirements for your target role and flags missing keywords.

📊

Impact Score

Rates each bullet point for use of action verbs, quantified results, and specificity — then suggests improvements.

📋

Section Completeness

Checks that all essential sections (contact info, summary, experience, education, skills) are present and complete.

✍️

Weak Phrasing Detector

Identifies passive language, clichés ('team player', 'results-driven'), and vague statements and rewrites them.

📏

Length & Formatting

Flags resumes that are too long, too short, or have inconsistent formatting that looks unprofessional.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my resume for free?

Open ApplyCraft, paste your resume content into the form fields, and use the AI Polish feature to get instant suggestions. The live preview shows you exactly how recruiters will see your document.

What is a good resume score?

A strong resume should include quantified achievements in every experience bullet, relevant keywords from the job posting, and clean formatting with no ATS-breaking elements.

Can the AI rewrite my resume for me?

Yes. The AI Polish feature rewrites your entire resume using strong action verbs and improved phrasing, tailored to your target role and language.

How often should I update my resume?

Update it every 3–6 months, or immediately after a major achievement. Keep it tailored to the specific role you're applying for.
