ApplyCraft offers optional AI helpers. This page explains exactly when AI is used, what is sent, who processes it, and its limits — so you can decide whether to use it.

AI is always optional. Building a resume or cover letter, choosing templates, previewing, and exporting PDF/DOCX never use AI. Nothing is sent to an AI provider until you click a specific AI button.

1. Features that use AI

  • Generate or draft resume content
  • Translate a resume to another language
  • Rewrite or improve an achievement bullet
  • ATS keyword suggestions
  • AI tailoring of your Master Profile to a job description (paid pass)

2. What is sent, and when

Only the text relevant to the action you trigger is sent — for example, the single bullet you ask to rewrite, or your resume plus the job description you paste for tailoring. It is sent only at the moment you click, through a server proxy we operate on Cloudflare, and is not collected for advertising or profiling.

3. Who processes it

AI requests are processed by Anthropic (the Claude model family). The text you submit is sent to Anthropic's API to produce the response and is handled under Anthropic's terms. Anthropic is based in the United States; see International transfers.

4. Data handling

We proxy AI requests to keep our API key secret and to apply abuse protection. We do not build a profile of you from AI inputs, and we do not store your AI inputs or outputs as part of your account beyond what is needed to return the response to your browser.

5. Limitations

  • AI output can be inaccurate, generic, or biased — always review and edit it before using it.
  • AI does not verify facts about you; never present AI text as true unless it is.
  • The "ApplyCraft ATS Readiness Score" is an internal heuristic, not an AI prediction and not a guarantee of passing any specific applicant tracking system.
  • We do not use AI to make automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you.
